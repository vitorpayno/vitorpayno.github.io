Claraboia

Graduation Final Project
Creative Direction, 3D, Motion Graphics, Music Production

Creative Direction, 3D, Motion Graphics, Music Production: Vitor Payno
Color palette: Giovani Flores

"Claraboia" is an animated music video I created as my Bachelor's degree final project. The song and video are centered around the theme of hypnagogia, the state of consciousness we enter just before deep sleep. The vibrant colors, abstract shapes, and music in the video symbolize the experiences one can have during moments of hypnagogia, when the mind is in a highly associative and exceptionally creative state.
