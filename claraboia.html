Claraboia

Bachelor Final Project
Creative Direction, 3D, Motion Graphics, Music
2016

Creative Direction, 3D, Motion Graphics, Music: Vitor Payno
Colours, Textures: Giovani Flores

Claraboia is an animated music video produced as the final project of my Bachelor degree in Social Communication. The main theme for both music and animation is hypnagogia – the moment between states of consciousness that happens when you’re about to fall asleep. The bright colours, abstract shapes and dreamlike music represent the sensations you can experience in a hypnagogic state, where the mind is in hyper-associative, ultra-creative state.
