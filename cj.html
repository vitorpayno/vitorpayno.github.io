Cidade Jardim

Client: Shopping Cidade Jardim
3D, Animation
2019

Creative Direction: MMMode
3D, Design: Pedro Veneziano
Animation, Compositing: Vitor Payno

Cidade Jardim is a high-fashion commercial center located in the city of São Paulo. Its new visual campaign was conceived based on a tale about magical garden creatures coming alive from the fusion between nature and technology. For this project, I was able to create animated pieces where the garden beings interact and have fun.
