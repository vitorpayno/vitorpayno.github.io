Potências Negras

Client: Profissas / Ana Minuto
Motion Design, Sound Design
2021

Visual Identity: Victor Lambertucci
Motion Design, Sound Design: Vitor Payno

Potências Negras is an ongoing project developed by Escola Profissas and Minuto Consultoria, dedicated to combatting racial disparities in the job market. I joined the project as a partner for the Potências Negras Summit event, creating the motion graphics and sound design for the main title, as well as crafting graphic animations for the pieces broadcasted during the event.
